Key Paradise ADA statement

Our hotel is committed to providing equal access and ensuring a seamless experience for all guests. Our staff is trained to assist guests with disabilities and provide any necessary accommodations. If you have specific accessibility requirements or any questions regarding our accessibility features, please contact us, and we will be happy to assist you.

Here are some of the measures we have implemented to ensure the best possible experience for our guests:
  • Accessible parking spaces located close to the entrance.
  • Wheelchair-accessible entrances and public areas.
  • ADA-compliant elevators for easy access to different floors.
  • TTY and hearing-impaired equipment available upon request.
  • Service animals are welcome.

Accessible rooms

Accessible Double

A double accessible hotel room is a specially designed accommodation option that caters to the needs of guests with accessibility requirements. These rooms are thoughtfully crafted to provide a comfortable and convenient stay for individuals with mobility challenges or disabilities. Featuring wider doorways and spacious layouts, they offer ample maneuvering space for wheelchairs or mobility devices. Additionally, these rooms may include accessible amenities such as grab bars in the bathroom, roll-in showers with seating, and lowered fixtures for easy reach. The double configuration in these rooms allows for shared accommodation, making them suitable for families or groups traveling together.
