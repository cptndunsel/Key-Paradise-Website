Accessibility features

At Key Paradise Hotel, we strive to ensure that all our guests have a comfortable and inclusive stay. Our hotel is equipped with a range of accessibility features to accommodate guests with diverse needs. We have wheelchair-accessible entrances, elevators, and designated parking spaces for easy mobility. We offer rooms that are designed with accessibility in mind, featuring spacious layouts, grab bars, and accessible bathroom facilities. Our staff is trained to provide assistance and support to guests with disabilities, ensuring that they have a seamless and enjoyable experience during their stay. We are committed to promoting accessibility and inclusivity, making Key Paradise Hotel a welcoming destination for all.

Please visit our accesibility page for more information. If you have questions or concerns, please Contact us.
